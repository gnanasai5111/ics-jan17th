Rugged Audio Video Recorder

An audio video recording contains both audio and video information. An audio video recording, however, includes both audio and video signals recorded together, ensuring synchronicity when the recording is played. Rugged Audio Video Recorder module is designed to capture and store DVI-I Video-In and Line-In audio signals in an interleaved fashion. It has a Video Bypass DVI-I Output port compatible to BARCO Display

Features

  • TI DaVinci Video Processors DM8148
  • Form Factor-6U VME
  • Configurable Video frame rate 25 to 60 frames/sec
  • Supports video bit rates 100 to 1000Kbits/Second
  • Audio channel configurable to stereo or mono input/output
  • Supports WAV and PCM audio encoding
  • Time stamping and bookmark facility for recorded data
  • Gigabit Ethernet interfaces
  • Supports video VESA Modes from 640x480 to 1920x1200 configurable , 24 bit true colors

Application

  • Communication networks
