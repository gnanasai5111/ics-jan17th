Trunk-Base Station

The DMR Trunk– BS mainly works as interface between two communication networks either wired or wireless. This works for trunking feature between different users. The Trunk has IP connectivity providing flexibility to connect to different network users / services via IP. The different modules involved in Base station with trunk are Antenna System comprises of three antennas each of 10 dBi gain covering full 360 deg HPBW, Channel Unit with RF Booster, Switches to route RF to and from different Antennae, filters, Diversity selection switches, controls redundancy switches, Transceiver Section, Baseband Processing and Interfaces Section built around FPGA SoC and interfaces to various devices through the designated interfaces, Other Interfaces: USB interface, GPS interface, Wi-Fi interface, Bluetooth Interface, Ethernet interface and Power Supply Section accepts input from 230V AC mains provides DC 24V. Alternately, 24 V DC is also accepted for the operation in case of no AC power availability. Auto changeover provision is built -in.
