Wide Band Digital Recorder

Wide Band Digital Recorder is intended to perform Recording and Replay of the intercepted at 70MHz IF with maximum bandwidth of 40MHz. It is realized in VME based modules suitable for packaging in 4U, 19” rack mountable chassis and is controlled through the Gigabit Ethernet LAN.  Controller provides user friendly options for Deletion of selected file/files, Analog/Digital Replay, Spectral display of live signal, Recording, Bandwidth selection, over write facility.

Features

  • VME based modules
  • Controlled through the Gigabit Ethernet LAN
  • 70 MHz IF channel
  • Maximum bandwidth of 40MHz
  • Recording duration is of 6 hours  
  • Data stored in COTS Solid State Disks 
  • Digital Replay of the recorded signals
