S-Band Power Amplifier

Solid state S - Band Driver operating over 2995.5 to 3001.5 MHz, has capability to deliver up to 200 Watts Pulse Power into 50 ohms load. Power Output level is indicated in a front panel display. The unit operates on 230V, 50Hz, Single phase, AC mains supply. Final amplifier stage has built in heat sink. Power Amplifier can be set to amplification when powered up and valid Trigger Input is fed. Status of the power amplifier is displayed on display. Fault indications are provided on front panel using LEDs.

Features

  • Control Card To Front panel interface For Frequency operation, Power level control
  • Fault conditions observed For PA shut OFF
  • Fault conditions are displayed
  • All R.F. modules Are mounted On A heat sink For better power dissipation
  • Thermal LED, VSWR LED, Trigger LED

Application

  • LINAC Systems

Specifications

Frequency tuning VCO based
Peak power output 200 Watts (typ) with 12 uSec Pulse width and 300 Hz PRF
Output pulse width 3 to 12 uSec
Accuracy +/-1 Watt

PRF 10 to 300 Hz
Impedance 50 Ohms
