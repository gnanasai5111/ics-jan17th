Transponder

The Transponder is used to process the down converted IFF band signal.Hardware architecture is based on Zynq ultra scale FPGA and high end ADC's to process the received signal. Zynq ultra scale FPGA Interfaced with DDR4 memory, ARINC, MIL1553 to support different standard communications. The signal processor of Transponder shall be configured with feature of DPSK and MSK demodulation in addition to the conventional pulse amplitude demodulation.

Features

  • Zynq ultra scale FPGA
  • ADC's of 500 MSPS
  • 128 Gb NAND MMC Flash
  • ARINC 429 Interface
  • MIL1553B Interface
  • USB3.0/USB2.0 Interface
  • 4 Gb DDR4 Memory
  • 10/100/1000 Ethernet Transceiver
  • VPX size form factor
  • 12 V DC power

Specifications
