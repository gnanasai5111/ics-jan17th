Mixer Module – HF

Mixer module is meant for operation in reception and transmission modes. In reception mode, the module selects, amplifies and converts the received radio signals in the operating frequency range from 2 to 24 MHz into signal of second intermediate frequency of 0.5 MHz. In transmission mode, the module amplifies and converts the arriving signals of IF 0.5 MHz into excitation voltage in operating frequency range from 2 to 24MHz.

Features

  • QT qualified and trial Flight tested
  • Used in HF Transceiver (B1KTsIIIM) of HF Communication System (R805-K3M)
  • It is based on double conversion receiver
  • Module Health check
  • Mixer Module is a mission critical system
  • Cooling shall be through natural convection/conduction cooling
  • Meets MIL STD 810G
  • No periodic maintenance required
  • MTBF of 2500Hrs at 25 deg C

Application

  • Airborne HF Radio
