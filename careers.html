Senior Wireless Baseband Algorithm Engineer

Senior Wireless Baseband Algorithm Engineer

Roles and Responsibilities

We are looking for Senior Baseband Engineer for wireless PHY team to lead the team in its efforts to design and develop PHY layer algorithms in proprietary wireless communications, as well as standard wireless communications such as 802.11ax/be/ac, 5G and beyond. The wireless baseband team is responsible for design of PHY algorithms as per specifications in MATLAB and port them to C and RTL.

You will be working in a fast-paced environment, operate gracefully under stress, handle multiple assignments in parallel. The person should be a team player as the work involves a lot of cross-functional aspects and you will need to show excellent interpersonal and understanding skills.

Minimum Qualifications
  • 7-8 years of hands-on experience in Baseband algorithm development
  • Strong experience in developing PHY algorithms and complete Tx, Rx chains in MATLAB
  • Knowledge of at least one PHY layer – 802.11, 5G, LTE, 3G etc.
  • Excellent signal processing knowledge and be able to mentor freshers
  • Good Debugging skills and tuning the algorithms to meet the stringent requirements of wireless communication algorithms
Additional Qualifications
  • Xilinx FPGA porting and verification is desired
  • System level design and development experience is highly preferred
Education & Experience
  • BTech in ECE in a reputed institute
  • SMTech in Communication related specialization in NIT, IIT, IIIT institutes
Senior Wireless Network Protocols Engineer

Senior Wireless Network Protocols Engineer

Roles and Responsibilities

We are looking for senior software engineer for the Networking team to lead the team in the architecture and development of the next generation standard wireless networking technology such 802.11ax/be/ac, 5G and proprietary wireless technologies. The wireless networking team is responsible for the design and development of MAC and above layers such as LLC, RLC, RRC and call control layers of wireless stack. The responsibilities include understanding the standard specifications, customer requirements and then design, develop and port the complete stack to Linux based hardware platforms .

You will be working in a fast-paced environment, operate gracefully under stress, handle multiple assignments in parallel. The person should be a team player as the work involves a lot of cross-functional aspects and you will need to show excellent interpersonal skills .

Minimum Qualifications
  • 7-8 years of hands-on experience in networking stacks
  •  You must have system level software design and development experience and have participated in full-life cycle of requirements gathering, design, development and testing of a complete wireless stack
  • You must possess knowledge of at least one MAC layer – 802.11, 5G, LTE, 3G etc.
  • Strong software design and development skills in C, C++ programming languages is essential
  • You should possess excellent debugging skills using Wireshark, gdb, etc.
Additional Qualifications
  • Call control, phone software development experience
  • Knowledge of VoIP protocols, SIP, RTP is highly preferred
  • Experience with Linux networking, including sockets API, is desired
