Laser range finder

Innovation Communication Systems have vast experience in design and development of LASER Electronics products. ICS has developed multiple products for LASER RANGE FINDER (LRF). The role LRF electronics (ICSLRF002) is implementation of range computation logic, controls to the LRF sub-units, interface with laser pointer and illuminator and RS-422.

Features

  • Spartan 6 series FPGA
  • STM Micro controller
  • PIN Photo Diode card is used to for converting electrical signal to optical signal
  • Photo Detector Card used for receiving laser signal
  • 30MHz reference clock
  • RS422 Interface
  • 512Kbyte flash memory
  • RS232 Interface
  • 24V DC Supply with EMI/EMI line filter
  • RTL (Application Code)
  • GUI Design & Integration
