The High Capacity Radio Relay—IRR-B4(HCRR) is indigenously designed and developed by ICS . It operates in Band –IV (4400 MHz to 5000 MHz) capable of Full-Duplex Data transfer up to 100 Mbps.

High Capacity Radio Relay—IRR-B4(HCRR) can be deployed for

1.Point to Point (P2P) Communication link , with data rates 2,8, 34 & 100 Mbps, for a range of 20 km in Fixed Frequency mode .
2. Point to Multi Point (P2MP) Communication Link of over 20km with 8 Mbps aggregated data rate for outstations.

  • In addition to P2P and P2MP modes, the system can also be configured to serve as a single hop repeater, with 2, 8, 34 & 100 Mbps data rates, for establishing Communication up to 35 km
  • The system has inbuilt ECCM features such as Frequency hopping, Frequency evasion, Adaptive Frequency Control and Adaptive Power Control
  • In frequency hopping mode, Communication over a range of 20km is possible with 8 Mbps Data Rate
  • The above features of IRR-B4 are also characterized by optimized transmit power and RF channel band width
  • System has E1, E3 and Ethernet base band interfaces, with both Electrical and Optical connectivity. System accepts 230 V AC and +48 V DC with auto changeover facility
  • System has Narrow beam antenna for P2P Communication and Sector Beam Antenna for P2MP Communication
