Leadership Team

Rama Rao Sreeramaneni

Founder and CEO

Rama completed his B.Tech at GITAM and M.S. at Univ. of Missouri. He started his career in the electronics division at Ford Motor Company before returning to India and starting ZSP India (ZSP was acquired by LSI Logic) which focused on DSP ASICs for cellular market.

Rama then co-founded Hellosoft (acquired by Imagination Technologies, UK) which focused on VoIP & Wireless IP and VoWiFi ASICs.

Post Hellosoft, Rama founded Uurmi (acquired by MathWorks) which focused on IP for autonomous cars and wireless communications. Rama worked at MathWorks for a brief stint before starting ICS.

Rama has over 30 yrs. of experience in leading development teams and sales in the field of communications and image processing.

Srinivas Bathini

VP Engineering

Bathini is the VP Engineering at ICS and has more than 35 yrs. of rich experience in Protocol stack, H/W design, and system design.

He completed his B.E and M.E at Osmania University. He started his career at ITI, India developing telephony switching systems and moved to Singapore to work at ST Electronics.

From ST Electronics, he moved to USA to work at Nortel Networks, Telcosoft, Texas Instruments and Armilliare Technologies in various positions managing large teams and then moved back to India as Director of engineering at Hellosoft (Acquired by Imagination Technologies, UK).

Post Hellosoft, he was VP of engineering at Uurmi (acquired by MathWorks) where he worked as Director of communication systems. Bathini worked at MathWorks for brief period as Director of engineering before joining at ICS.

Dr. Yoganandam

Sr. Director Technical

Dr. Yoganandam is the Sr. Director Technical at ICS. He has a combined experience of more than 42 years across academics and aeronautics, telecommunications product industries. He has published several journals and conference papers and has one patent to his credit.

He has completed his B.E from JNTU, M.E at IIT-Kharagpur and Ph.D. from Osmania University (OU). Dr. Yoganandam started his career at HAL and went on to work at the R & T Unit for Navigational Electronics, Osmania University (OU) where he subsequently rose to the level of Director. For a couple of years, he was also associated with the Signal Processing research group at Electrical and Computer Engineering department, Concordia University, Montreal, Canada .

From OU he joined ZSP India (ZSP was acquired by LSI Logic) and then moved to Hellosoft (acquired by Imagination Technologies, UK) as Technical Director .

Post Hellosoft, he went to work as HOD (Chairman) of EE department at BITS Pilani, Hyderabad before joining ICS.

Srinivas Gupta

Director RF

Mr Gupta is Director RF at ICS and has more than 40 years of experience and has been delivering mission critical RF, Microwave and Embedded Systems and subsystems.

He did B.Tech at NIT Warangal and M.Tech at JNTU, Hyderabad.

Mr Gupta started his career at Avionics Design Bureau, HAL, Hyderabad and then moved to ECIL as Sr. Manager. He has also taken up multiple consultancy assignments in his career. He worked as Sr.VP/COO at Axis Aerospace and Technologies.

Mr Gupta is the founder and CEO of Raaga Associates which is acquired by ICS.

Buchhi Babu

Director Hardware

Buchhi Babu is the Director Hardware at ICS and has 30+ years of experience in sub systems and systems design for mission critical applications.

He has completed his BE from Pune university and started carrier at Pennar Electronics and moved to ARM and Chiplogic (acquired by Analog Devices Inc.)

At Analog Devices Inc. he was responsible for the reference platforms in India and US.

Buchhi Babu was the promoter and managing director at Vrinda Technologies providing subsystem solutions for Defence and Aerospace before joining ICS.
