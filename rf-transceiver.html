RF Transceiver module

The RF module operating in the frequency range of 30 MHz to 512 MHz consists of three sections (i) Transceiver assembly comprising of transmitter, receiver, AGC, ALC, hopping Filter, Rx filter bank (ii) power amplifier and (iii) harmonic filter assembly comprising of filter bank, high power solid state switches, dual directional coupler and T/R switch. synthesizer, part of transceiver assembly, comprises Fast hopping frequency synthesizer, reference oscillator. In addition to the above, the RF module has dedicated regulators and LDOs for each assembly.

RF Transceiver Module Features

RF Transceiver Module Features

  • All solid-state design. Broadband design
  • High power filter bank for harmonics rejection
  • Solid state switches for fast switching (50μs)
  • Fast hopping synthesizer with settling time of 50 μs
  • Suitable for Cc-location applications
  • Selectable channel bandwidth for NB and WB services
  • Analog and digital modulations support
  • VPX form factor transceiver
  • Meets MIL STDs for vehicular installation
  • Meets requirements of SDR with SCA compliance
